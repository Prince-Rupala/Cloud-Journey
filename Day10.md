## What SSH Does

It lets you safely connect to and control a remote computer or server from my own laptop

## Difference Between Public and Private Key

You leave you Public key (the lock) out in the open on the remote server, and you keep your Private key (the key) hidden on your laptop.

## Why Key Authentication Is More Secure

Impossible to guess: An SSH key is a massive, complex mathematical string. A hacker's bot can guess a weak password in seconds, but guessing an SSH key would take billions of years.

No typing required: Because you don't type anything, sneaky malware on your computer (like keyloggers) cannot spy on your keyboard to steal your login details.

## What Is Localhost?

Local host is the built-in nickname for own computer.
